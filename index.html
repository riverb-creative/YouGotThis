Why do You Got This?

Since I started the Full Stack Web Development program, I have been adament on creating a more accessible world. Having accessibility in the digital world we live in, is an important aspect to provide to everyone..

Unfortunately, accessibility is an afterthought to many creators in multitude of products that are targeted to the general consumer. Disabled in today's world has a negative connotation, while asking for accessibility is seen as a weakness. I want to change the thought process of how individuals view these words. I often use a screen-reader because it helps me to listen and read at the same time. We should be viewing accessibility as convenient; what if a person just doesn't want to read this webpage? This individual could use a screen-reader embedded into this webpage.

The amount of vast ideas I have for accessible platforms that could be created for anyone to use are endless.

Accessibility Ideas:

  • Create an embedded text-to-speech reader
  • Multiple options of different ways to display words such as:
    • Dyslexie
    • Bionic Reading
  • Different embedded website themes:
    • Dark Themes
    • Light Themes
    • High Contrast Themes
